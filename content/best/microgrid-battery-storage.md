- **Configurability** - Sizing storage to local generation and critical loads.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is a microgrid battery storage system?
A microgrid battery storage system stores energy locally so a microgrid can balance on-site generation and loads, and continue operating independently from the main grid during outages or planned islanding.

### How does storage help a microgrid?
Storage smooths variable renewable output, supplies power when local generation is insufficient, and enables a microgrid to island, keeping critical facilities running without grid power.

### Can I deploy a temporary microgrid?
Yes. Rental, plug-and-play systems such as Aggreko's can establish temporary or rapidly deployed microgrids for events, construction, disaster recovery, or remote operations.
