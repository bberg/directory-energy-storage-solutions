## What Sets Utility-Scale Storage Apart

- **Scale** - Projects measured in megawatts and gigawatt-hours, not kilowatts.
- **Grid services** - Frequency regulation, capacity, and renewable firming.
- **Long project timelines** - Interconnection, permitting, and EPC complexity.
- **Software** - Dispatch and optimization platforms are central to returns.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What counts as utility-scale energy storage?
Utility-scale (or grid-scale) storage typically refers to front-of-meter systems large enough to provide grid services, generally rated in megawatts with megawatt-hours of capacity, often connected at transmission or distribution level.

### Which companies lead in utility-scale BESS?
Fluence and Wärtsilä are among the most established turnkey grid-scale providers, while integrated utilities like RWE and engineering firms like Siemens Energy and Ameresco develop and operate large projects directly.

### Do utility-scale providers include software?
Many do. Wärtsilä and Fluence pair hardware with energy management and dispatch software, and dedicated platforms can optimize asset performance across a portfolio.
