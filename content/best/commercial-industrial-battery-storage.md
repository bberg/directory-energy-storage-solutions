## Why Commercial & Industrial Sites Add Battery Storage

- **Demand charge reduction** - Discharge during peak periods to lower utility demand charges.
- **Load shifting** - Store cheap off-peak energy and use it when prices are high.
- **Backup power** - Keep critical processes running through outages.
- **Solar self-consumption** - Capture excess on-site generation for later use.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How big is a typical C&I battery storage system?
Commercial and industrial systems commonly range from tens of kilowatt-hours for small businesses to several megawatt-hours for large factories. Many providers offer configurable or containerized systems that scale with the site.

### Should I buy or rent commercial battery storage?
Rental options, such as Aggreko's plug-and-play units, suit temporary needs, fast deployment, or projects testing storage before committing to capital. Owned systems usually make more sense for permanent demand charge or resilience strategies.

### Can battery storage pair with my existing solar?
Yes. Most C&I storage systems are designed to integrate with on-site solar to increase self-consumption and provide backup, and several providers also supply compatible inverters and controls.
