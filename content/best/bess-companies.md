## How to Choose a BESS Company

- **Project scale** - Match the provider to your size, from C&I to gigawatt-scale utility.
- **Hardware vs. software** - Some companies build systems, others optimize them; many do both.
- **Battery chemistry** - Lithium-ion is common, but iron flow, gravity, and hydrogen suit longer durations.
- **Delivery model** - Choose between product manufacturers, design-build integrators, rentals, and full-service developers.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is a BESS company?
A BESS (battery energy storage system) company designs, manufactures, integrates, or operates systems that store electricity for later use, supporting peak shaving, backup power, renewable integration, and grid services.

### Who are the largest BESS providers?
Fluence and Wärtsilä are among the most established utility-scale providers, while companies like Honeywell, Siemens Energy, and RWE bring large industrial and utility experience. Specialists like Energy Vault and ESS, Inc. focus on long-duration storage.

### What is the difference between a BESS manufacturer and integrator?
Manufacturers build the hardware (battery enclosures, inverters, controls), while integrators and design-build firms combine components into a complete system tailored to a site. Many leading companies do both.
