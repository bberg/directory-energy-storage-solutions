## What Energy Storage Software Does

- **Monitoring** - Real-time visibility into state of charge, health, and performance.
- **Optimization** - Dispatching to maximize savings, revenue, or grid services.
- **Control** - Coordinating storage with solar, loads, and the grid.
- **Portfolio management** - Operating multiple assets across sites as one fleet.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is energy storage management software?
It is the control and optimization layer that decides when a battery charges and discharges, monitors system health, and integrates storage with solar, loads, and market signals to maximize value.

### Can I buy storage software separately from hardware?
Yes. Software-first providers such as Stem offer platforms that work across hardware, while companies like Fluence, Wärtsilä, and Energy Vault pair their own software with their systems.

### How does AI improve storage performance?
AI-driven platforms forecast load, prices, and generation, then optimize dispatch automatically, capturing more value from demand charge reduction, energy arbitrage, and grid services than manual scheduling.
