## Fluence Overview

A market leader in BESS with a comprehensive ecosystem of scalable products, services, and digital applications, spanning utility-scale solutions down to plug-and-play commercial offerings.

**Best For:**
- Utilities and large developers
- Projects wanting a full product-to-software ecosystem
- Buyers who also need commercial or microgrid options

**Key Strengths:**
- Broad, configurable product range
- Digital applications for optimization and trading
- Reach from commercial to grid scale

## Wärtsilä Overview

Turnkey utility-scale and grid-scale BESS products paired with energy management software to operate and optimize them.

**Best For:**
- Utility-scale and grid-scale projects
- Buyers prioritizing integrated hardware-plus-software
- Renewable firming and grid-services applications

**Key Strengths:**
- Turnkey grid-scale delivery
- Mature energy management software
- Strong utility and power-systems heritage

## Which Should You Choose?

### Choose Fluence if:
- You want a single ecosystem spanning commercial to utility scale
- Digital optimization and trading applications matter
- You may also need plug-and-play or microgrid systems

### Choose Wärtsilä if:
- Your focus is purely utility-scale or grid-scale
- You want tightly integrated turnkey hardware and software
- You value deep power-systems and energy-management expertise

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Are Fluence and Wärtsilä direct competitors?
Yes. Both are leading turnkey providers of utility-scale and grid-scale battery energy storage with their own management software, making them direct competitors for large grid projects.

### What is the main difference between them?
Fluence offers a broader range that extends down to commercial, plug-and-play, and microgrid systems, while Wärtsilä concentrates on turnkey utility-scale and grid-scale BESS with integrated energy management software.

### Do both provide software?
Yes. Both pair hardware with energy management software for monitoring, dispatch, and optimization of storage assets.
