# Top Fluence Alternatives & Competitors in 2026

Fluence is a market leader in battery energy storage, but it is not the only option. Whether you need a different technology, a software-first approach, or a particular delivery model, these are the top Fluence alternatives and competitors.

## How to Pick a Fluence Alternative

- **Utility-scale parity** - Wärtsilä is the most direct turnkey grid-scale competitor.
- **Software-first** - Stem leads if optimization software matters more than owning hardware.
- **Long duration** - Energy Vault and ESS, Inc. offer non-lithium long-duration options.
- **Flexibility** - Aggreko's rental model suits temporary or rapidly deployed needs.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Who are Fluence's main competitors?
For utility-scale storage, Wärtsilä is the most direct competitor, with Honeywell, Siemens Energy, and integrated utilities also competing. Stem competes on the software layer, while Energy Vault and ESS, Inc. compete in long-duration storage.

### What is the best alternative to Fluence for utility-scale projects?
Wärtsilä is the closest alternative, providing turnkey utility-scale and grid-scale BESS with integrated energy management software.

### Is there a software-only alternative to Fluence?
Yes. Stem offers AI-powered energy storage management software focused on optimizing and controlling clean energy portfolios, rather than supplying the hardware itself.
